Orvis promo codes - 15% OFF in July 2024

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, September 16, 2024

FAQs

Does Orvis have free shipping?

You can get free standard shipping at Orvis with their Orvis Rewards Visa credit card. Alternatively, you can get free standard shipping when you order $50 or more. Standard shipping will cost $6.95 for orders of $25 or less. Check this Marie Claire page as well for a free shipping Orvis promo code. 

How do I get 15% off Orvis?

Sign up for the Orvis newsletter emails and you will get 15% Orvis coupon code to save on your first order. You’ll also be sent exclusive discounts, sales updates, and brand news. To sign up, go to the Orvis website and type your preferred email address into their sign-up box.  

Is there an Orvis promo code for students?

Unfortunately, Orvis doesn't have a student discount code at this time. Should this change, we will update you with the relevant information to help you save on your next Orvis purchase.

Does Orvis have Black Friday sales?

Yes. Shop for the best savings during the Orvis Black Friday sales. Whether you’re purchasing clothing or fishing reels, you can get a discount during this time. Be sure to check this page again in November for the latest sales updates. We’ve previously seen up to 50% off in the Orvis Black Friday sales. 

What is the return policy at Orvis?

Orvis has a 100% guarantee for all its products. This means you can return your item if you are not happy. To make a return for online shopping, you will need to use their returns portal. You will also need your order number and zip code. It’s worth noting the $6.95 return shipping label will be deducted from your refund total.  

Hints and Tips

Save on Style: Spend less on your next shop with their dedicated Sale page with discounts of up to 65% on everything from fishing clothing to tent sales. There are sections for both men’s clothing and women’s clothing too. You can sort the sale by product types such as sweatshirts, jeans, and tops. By shopping the sale, you’re already getting a fantastic saving but they also let you use an Orvis promo code on sale items for a double saving. 

Stay Social: If you’re always searching social media for outdoor outfits, make sure to follow Orvis on Instagram. You can see the latest products and keep clued in on their conservation efforts too. We particularly recommend their ‘Moments of Chill’ series, designed to help you take a moment to relax. You can also follow Orvis on Facebook or Twitter. It’s always worth giving these pages a follow as you might get the occasional promo code or free shipping promotion. 

Join Orvis Rewards: You can sign up for Orvis Rewards loyalty program to get exclusive discounts on your order. However, you will need to sign up for their Visa credit card to join the loyalty program. Once you’ve signed up, you should get $25 off your purchase the same day. The loyalty program means you can earn points on your Orvis orders. You’ll earn 3 points per $1 spent at Orvis. As this is a credit card, you can also spend $1 dollar at other stores and earn 1 point. The best part? You will also get free standard shipping on every order. It’s worth knowing as standard shipping usually costs $9.95. 

Newsletter Promotions: Sign up for the newsletter at Orvis and you can get 15% off on your first order. You’ll also be sent the latest sales and promotions on relevant clothing, home, and sport products. Whether you’re shopping for sweaters or flannel shirts, signing up for the newsletter will help you save on your Orvis order. To sign up for the newsletter, go to the Orvis homepage and scroll down to ‘Enjoy 15% Off Your Order Today’. You can then type your preferred email address into the box and click ‘Sign Up’. Orvis should send your exclusive 15% off promo code to your email. It’s worth noting this promotion cannot be used on any fishing products. 

Deals of the Day: Orvis has a ‘Best Deals of the Day’ section which you can find on their homepage. It can be difficult to find. However, simply click the small green ‘Offers’ tag above on the right hand side of the page. You’ll be able to shop the deals Orvis recommends as their best at the time. Previous examples have included Buy 2 Ultimate Khakis and Save $50. Of course, you can check this page too for the best sales and promotions.
