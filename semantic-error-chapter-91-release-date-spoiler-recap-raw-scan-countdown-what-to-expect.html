Sangwoo rushed to the hospital, leaving Jaeyoung behind. He was worried about his father’s condition, but also felt guilty for lying to his parents about his relationship with Jaeyoung. He wondered if he should tell them the truth, or if he should break up with Jaeyoung to avoid hurting them.

Meanwhile, Jaeyoung waited for Sangwoo to come back, but he didn’t receive any news from him. He was worried about Sangwoo’s father, but also felt hurt and confused by Sangwoo’s sudden departure. He wondered if Sangwoo still loved him, or if he was regretting their relationship.
