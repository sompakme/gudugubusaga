See All the Best 2022 Oscars After-Party Looks

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, July 20, 2024

Well, the 94th Academy Awards proved to be quite a historical night. But besides the obvious drama that ensued during the ceremony and all the deserving winners, last night's fashion was truly the main event.

Following the televised show, attendees, nominees, and champions alike changed outfits and dispersed across Los Angeles for the after parties, like the famous Vanity Fair Oscar After-Party and the Governors Ball. And after two years of virtual awards shows, it seemed like the celebrities came ready to party.

Here are all the best looks from the hottest soirées in town following a night to truly never forget — and we'd know, because we were there.
