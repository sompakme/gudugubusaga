Mike Tyson and Terry Crews Kill it on Lip Sync Battle: Who Won???

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, August 1, 2024

Terry Crews’ epic performance of Vanessa Carlton’s “A Thousand Miles” on Lip Sync Battle might challenge Anne Hathaway’s performance of Wrecking Ball.

The Who Wants to Be a Millionaire host and former Brooklyn Nine-Nine start competed against Mike Tyson, singing Carlton’s hit song.

That’s the only competition against Mike Tyson I’d want to be in.

Although Tyson was entertaining while lip syncing "Push It" in some seriously tight black pants, Crews schooled him.

Lip syncing, twirling, and dancing with ribbons, Crews killed it when singing the 2000 hit. Even co-host Chrissy Teigen couldn’t resist joining in on the ribbon dancing.

This isn’t the first time Crews has performed this song.

When he appeared in the film White Chicks, he didn’t just lip sync "A Thousand Miles" … he sang it.

His character, Latrell, busted out the song while on a date and even did interpretive dance based upon the lyrics.

Crews’ talents aren’t limited to just singing Vanessa Carlton songs.

He also proved his is a serious “nip syncer.”

On The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon, he bared his chest and let his nipples “talk,” while Fallon used a green screen to appear more muscular.

They then let their pecs do the singing and performed Paul McCartney and Stevie Wonder’s hit song “Ebony and Ivory.” He is a man of so many talents.
