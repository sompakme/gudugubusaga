Jason Heyward’s Early Life

Jason Heyward was born on August 9, 1989, in Ridgewood, New Jersey. He was raised in McDonough, Georgia, where he developed a passion for baseball at a young age. His talent was evident, and he was drafted by the Atlanta Braves in the 2007 MLB Draft right out of high school.

Professional Career

Heyward’s professional career began with the Atlanta Braves, where he played from 2010 to 2014. His performance was impressive, earning him a spot in the 2010 All-Star Game during his rookie season. In 2015, he was traded to the St. Louis Cardinals, where he played for a year before signing an eight-year contract with the Chicago Cubs in 2016.

Contract with the Chicago Cubs
