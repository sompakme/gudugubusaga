Shani Louk having fun and dancing before being kidnapped and killed by Hamas

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, July 3, 2024

On October 7, at around 6:30 local time, the Palestinian terrorist group Hamas launched a surprise attack on Israel and rockets began flying. Hamas claims to have fired 5,000 in the first round, Israel says it was half that.

A music festival being held near the border with the Gaza Strip, which is Palestinian territory, was attacked by Hamas and claimed the lives of at least 260 people.

The 30-year-old German woman, Shani Louk, was enjoying the music festival, dancing and having fun with her friends over the weekend, completely oblivious to the tragedy at hand. At the time, Shani could not have imagined that, in a matter of minutes, terrorists would carry out a devastating attack that would result in the loss of life and the kidnapping of hundreds of people at the festival.

Who was Shani Louk?

Shani Louk was involved in various lifestyle and fashion related activities. She was an expert in creating "dreadlocks", a tattoo artist and influencer.

A video of Shani being transported as a war trophy caused outrage around the world. The use of Shani's body for propaganda purposes highlights the brutality of the perpetrators and the lack of respect for human dignity.
