I Think About You Every Day: Davido Celebrates Late Bodyguard TJ on Posthumous Birthday

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, September 14, 2024

- March 23, 2021, marked the first posthumous birthday of Ogbeide Tijani Olamilekan, Davido's late bodyguard

- The singer took to his Instagram page to celebrate Tijani whom he fondly called Uncle TJ

- Tijani who had been ill for a while died in the early hours of Tuesday, December 8, 2020

Nigerian singer David Adeleke, also known as Davido, has taken to social media to celebrate his late bodyguard, Ogbeide Tijani Olamilekan.

On March 23, 2021, the singer took to his Instagram Story to honour Tijani on his posthumous birthday.

Sharing a photo of Tijani who he fondly referred to as 'Uncle TJ' in the post, he accompanied it with a loving caption expressing how much he missed him.
