Porn Star Lethal Lipps Sets Up GoFundMe Page Following Horrific Car Crash

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, September 18, 2024

Porn star Lethal Lipps, whose real name is Monique DeClou, has set up a GoFundMe page following a devastating car crash that left her with some serious injuries. 

The incident occurred on a North Carolina freeway, where DeClou was partially ejected through her windshield resulting in significant scarring. She is trying to raise $15,000 to cover medical costs for a thigh and facial bone reconstruction. 

According to her GoFundMe page, DeClou notes how she is "trying to raise this money to have the necessary surgeries to keep the appearance of my eye socket and thigh. Cosmetically appealing the Drs have so far done right by me as for making me sure I’m out of harm’s way… but I do not have enough to remove my insecurities it is not a mandatory surgery in the Drs eyes but .. I don’t know if I would be able to be comfortable with that. To be able to sustain me as I will not be able to work at this time thank you."
