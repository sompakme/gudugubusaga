Urban Decay is releasing a make-up collection inspired by Game of Thrones

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, August 19, 2024

Updated, 30/03/19

Urban Decay has released details and imagery of its entire Game of Thrones-inspired collection launching on 14 April.

The line-up includes an eyeshadow brush inspired by Arya Stark's Needle and Jon Snow's Longclaw, as well as a palette with a pop-up Iron Throne. There is also a Vault housing the entire collection costing £198.

Original article, 25/02/19

Whether you're counting down the days until the release of the last season of Game of Thrones in April or not, it's certainly no stretch to say that it's transformed our television screens over the course of its eight-year run. Now, the series is set to have an impact on our make-up bags too, with the announcement that Urban Decay is launching a cosmetics collection inspired by HBO's award-winning series.

Urban Decay broke the news via its Instagram channel, with visuals depicting two powerful-looking women wearing graphic eye make-up looks.

Think regal bronze shades worthy of a queen like Cersei, or high-pigmented cobalt colours to channel your own pretty take on a White Walker.

While the brand is keeping any further information about the limited-edition launch as closely guarded as the Game of Thrones plot twists, we predict from the promotional imagery at least a couple eyeshadows to rival the new Naked Reloaded palette and perhaps some metallic lip colour shades, too.

In fact, from the caption "Two Worlds Collide", we wouldn't be surprised if the new launch is a combination of high-pigment fiery red and icy blue eye colour shades in a combination of both matte and metallic finishes, in a nod to the original title of the book series; A Song of Ice and Fire.

Whatever the new launches are though, we're sure they will sell out fast. The only downside is that the collection isn't available until April - when the final series of Game of Thrones also airs.
