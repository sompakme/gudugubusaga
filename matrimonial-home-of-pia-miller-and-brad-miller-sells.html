Matrimonial home of Pia Miller and Brad Miller sells

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, August 29, 2024

PIA MILLER can move on from her former married life in Melbourne, as the sale of her matrimonial home was signed off this week by her ex, the retired AFL star Brad Miller.

Miller recently told Sydney Confidential that acting is still her focus as she continues to enjoy being a mother to her two boys.

The former Home And Away star is set to appear in the upcoming launch of the Channel 9 eight part crime thriller, Bite Club.

The actor, who is engaged to filmmaker and former model Tyson Mullane, has two children from previous relationships.

“Everything I do is for my kids. Every decision I make,” Miller, the ambassador for Melbourne Fashion Week, recently said.

She says her style — jeans, easy T-shirts, boots with more than a lean helping of black — has been informed by Melbourne’s prevailing outlook.

“I am definitely your typical Melbourne, wears-a-lot-of-black, kind of person,” she told Vogue.

Pia Miller joined the Sydney northern beaches-based Channel 7 television series in 2014 as the police officer Katarina Chapman.

She was on the show for three years, leaving last December.

Mullane has worked on various projects including the Tropfest award-winning film, Red Nuts.

Her former home in Melbourne’s Elwood sold for $1,165,000 with the settlement papers lodged this week.

It had been bought in 2005 by the then Melbourne Demons AFL star Brad Miller, two years before their marriage, and one year before the birth of their son.

It was a two bedroom 2004-built townhouse that cost $540,000 in 2005.

Brad Miller is currently the assistant coach for NEAFL team the Greater Western Sydney Giants.
