What is the purpose of placing flowers on a grave?

What is the reason for families to strew dirt on coffins?

A common conclusion to funeral services is for family members, relatives, and close friends of the dead to toss a handful of earth or soil upon his or her casket. Simply said, this is meant to represent the deceased’s return to his or her place of origin — man originates from the earth, and as such, he or she must return to the soil.
