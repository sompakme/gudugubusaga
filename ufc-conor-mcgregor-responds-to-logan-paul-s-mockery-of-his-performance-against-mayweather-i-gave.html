Conor McGregor got tangled up with Logan Paul in a boxing argument, where the Irishman threw a knockout punch at the youtuber.

Dillon Danis will make his boxing debut against KSI. Although the Irishman insists he supports his friend "all the way," he has no interest in the fight itself.

He tweeted, "I don't care. He should go back to doing mma if he's doing this little burger fight with you [KSI]."

Paul took the words of McGregor, who claimed to have had a better performance against Mayweather, to heart.

When would Conor return to fight?

Dana White recently gave an interview to ESPN and spoke about the future of Cooc McGregor, for him, the Irishman has not yet retired and expects his return to the octagon soon.

White confirmed: 'He [McGregor] is not going to retire anytime soon but he can see the light at the end of the tunnel'.

Michael Chandler would be the Irishman's possible opponent.

Chandler has actually lost three of his last four fights, but is still seen as a real threat to McGregor given his vicious punching style and heavy hands.

Conor would return to the octagon in the first quarter of 2023.
