Release Date, Cast, Trailer, OTT, Songs

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, July 30, 2024

Chaari 111 Movie (2024): Chaari 111 is an upcoming Telugu spy action comedy film starring Vennela Kishore in the lead role. The film is written and directed by T.G. Keerthi Kumar, who previously helmed the Sumanth-starrer Malli Modalaindi. Samyukta Viswanathan plays the female lead opposite Vennela Kishore. The film also features Murali Sharma, Raghu Babu, Prudhvi Raj, and Satya in supporting roles. The film is produced by Suresh Babu under the banner of Suresh Productions.

Release Date of Chaari 111 Movie

Chaari 111 is slated to hit the screens on March 15, 2024, which is a festive occasion for the Telugu audience. The film will clash with two other big releases, namely RRR and Pushpa, which are also releasing on the same day. RRR is a magnum opus directed by S.S. Rajamouli, starring N.T. Rama Rao Jr., Ram Charan, Alia Bhatt, and Ajay Devgn. Pushpa is an action thriller directed by Sukumar, starring Allu Arjun, Rashmika Mandanna, and Fahadh Faasil.

Both these films are expected to create a huge buzz at the box office and pose a tough competition for Chaari 111. However, the makers of this movie are confident that their film will find its audience and entertain them with its unique genre and comedy.

Plot Summary

The film revolves around Chaari, a timid and clumsy accountant who works for a secret spy agency. He is assigned to a mission to infiltrate a terrorist group and stop their plan to detonate a nuclear bomb. However, he soon realizes that he is way out of his league and has to rely on his wit and luck to survive. Along the way, he also falls in love with a beautiful journalist who is investigating the same case.

Trailer

The official trailer of Chaari 111 Movie was released on October 15, 2023, on the occasion of Vennela Kishore’s birthday. The trailer showcases the hilarious antics of Chaari as he tries to act like a spy and gets into trouble. The trailer also hints at some thrilling action sequences and a romantic track between Chaari and Samyukta. Official OTT Platform

Chaari 111 Movie is scheduled to be released theatrically on March 15, 2024, coinciding with the Ugadi festival. The makers have not announced any plans to release the film on any OTT platform as of now. However, given the popularity of OTT platforms in India and the uncertainty of the pandemic situation, there is a possibility that the film might have a digital premiere after its theatrical run.

Songs

The music of Chaari 111 Movie is composed by Simon K King, who has previously worked on films like Kolaigaran, Zombie, and Eeswaran. The film has four songs, which are penned by Ramajogayya Sastry, Bhaskarabhatla, and Krishna Kanth. The songs are sung by popular singers like Sid Sriram, Chinmayi, Anurag Kulkarni, and Haricharan. The audio launch of the film was held on November 10, 2023, at a grand event in Hyderabad. The songs have received a good response from the listeners and have been trending on various music platforms.

Review

As of November 17, 2023, the film has not been released yet and hence there are no reviews available from the critics or the audience. However, based on the trailer and the songs, the film looks promising and has generated a lot of curiosity among the viewers. The film seems to have a good mix of comedy, action, romance, and suspense, which are the ingredients for a successful commercial entertainer.

Vennela Kishore, who is known for his comic timing and expressions, seems to have nailed the role of Chaari and is expected to deliver a memorable performance. Samyukta Viswanathan, who is making her debut in Telugu cinema, looks gorgeous and shares a good chemistry with Vennela Kishore.

The supporting cast, especially Murali Sharma and Raghu Babu, also seem to have done justice to their roles. The director, T.G. Keerthi Kumar, has shown his flair for writing and directing a spy comedy, which is a rare genre in Telugu cinema. The music, cinematography, editing, and production values also look impressive and add to the appeal of the film. Overall, Chaari 111 seems to be a fun-filled and entertaining film that will appeal to all kinds of audiences and provide them with a wholesome cinematic experience.
