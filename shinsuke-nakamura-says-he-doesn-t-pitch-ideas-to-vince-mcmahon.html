Shinsuke Nakamura Says He Doesnt Pitch Ideas To Vince McMahon

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, September 15, 2024

Shinsuke Nakamura has been around WWE long enough to get to know Vince McMahon. He could get in there and pitch ideas for himself, but that doesn’t happen very often.

While speaking to Oral Sessions, Nakamura admitted that he doesn’t go in and speak with Vince McMahon very often. He does talk with The Chairman a bit before the match, but that is the extent of it.

I don’t pitch ideas to him [Vince McMahon] a lot. We talk after the match a little bit or before the match, if he [has] an idea for my promo.

When it comes to doing promos, Shinsuke Nakamura admitted that he still gets a bit nervous when doing interview in English. He has learned the language after being in America for so long, but that doesn’t help the tension that he feels when he’s about to speak it in public.

Yes [I get nervous], still. Nowadays, they only give me a short line. For example, a famous joke. I don’t know because I didn’t grow up in [the] US. That’s a hard thing for me.

Shinsuke Nakamura previously admitted that he still feels a struggle when wrestling WWE’s style. The King Of Strong Style entered WWE from a very different work in New Japan. It seems that he is still adjusting to a certain degree all this time later.
