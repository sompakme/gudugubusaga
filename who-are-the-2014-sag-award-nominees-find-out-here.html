Who Are the 2014 SAG Award Nominees? Find Out Here!

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, August 16, 2024

Roll out the red carpet, awards season is almost upon us! The nominations for the 20th Annual Screen Actors Guild Awards were announced Wednesday morning, and with a stylish list like this one, this may just be one of the most fashionable awards seasons yet.

Cate Blanchett, Sandra Bullock, Judi Dench, Meryl Streep and Emma Thompson are up for Outstanding Performance by a Female Actor in a Leading Role, while Jennifer Lawrence, Lupita Nyong'O, Julia Roberts, and many more fashionable faces have also scored nominations. Dapper gentlemen Matthew McConaughey and Tom Hanks also made the list for Outstanding Performance by a Male Actor in a Leading Role.

For full coverage, check out People.com’s SAG Awards recap, and make sure to tune in on January 18, 2014 to watch the show live.

Plus, keep your eyes peeled tomorrow morning, when the annual Golden Globe nominations are announced live by Olivia Wilde, Aziz Ansari, and Zoe Saldana on NBC's Today show at 8:38 a.m. EST.
