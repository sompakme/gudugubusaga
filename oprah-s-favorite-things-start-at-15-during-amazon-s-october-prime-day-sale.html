Oprahs Favorite Things Start at $15 During Amazon's October Prime Day Sale

If there’s one person I trust to curate my shopping list, it’s Oprah (obviously). That’s why, this October Prime Day, I’m turning to the mogul’s favorite Amazon finds. Luckily, a number of her go-to fashion and beauty products are on sale during the early Black Friday savings event. So, I narrowed down the top eight picks worth snagging during Prime Big Deal Days — and prices start at just $15. 

Below, you’ll find clothes, shoes, accessories, and beauty products all pulled from Oprah’s Favorite Things lists over the past few years. Snag her go-to flattering jeans, the anti-aging skincare pick she swears by, and the fall-perfect slippers that earned her seal of approval. The best part? You can score deals for up to 37 percent off; but you’ll want to act fast, the shopping marathon ends tomorrow.     

Best Oprah-Loved Amazon Prime Day Deals:

NYDJ Misses Marilyn Jeans

Speaking of major sales, these NYDJ jeans are now $50 instead of their original $109 price tag. Travel plans on the calendar? Then you won’t want to miss this handy, $14 jewelry storage box — aka the solution to tangled necklaces and missing earrings. Shop the bootie slippers in six autumnal tones at Amazon while they’re still marked down to $32.  

Another Oprah-approved Amazon find, the Sunday Riley Good Genes All-in-One Lactic Acid Face Serum is now on sale for $30.
