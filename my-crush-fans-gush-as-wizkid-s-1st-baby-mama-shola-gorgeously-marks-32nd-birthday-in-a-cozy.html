My Crush: Fans Gush As Wizkids 1st Baby Mama Shola Gorgeously Marks 32nd Birthday in a Cozy

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, July 26, 2024
  • Ogudu Oluwanishola, the mother of Wizkid’s first child, was incredibly happy as she celebrated her birthday in style
  • The delighted entrepreneur and mother of one shared an adorable birthday video on her Instagram page to commemorate her special day
  • Fans of the Grammy Award-winning musician couldn't help but show their love for his first baby mama

Oluwanishola Ogudu, the first baby mama of Afrobeats star Wizkid, celebrated her new age on April 19, 2023.

The single mother of one, who clocked 32, showed her gratitude for having made it this far in life by posting a cozy video of herself on her Instagram page.

Ogudu is the mother of Wizkid’s first child Boluwatife. In her caption, she wrote:

"Happy Birthday to ME!!! 32 where!!??? Forever 23, baby!!! ‍♀️❤️ #BlessedAndHighlyFavored."
