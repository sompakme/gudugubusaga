I can't have kids until Im confident that I can settle - Burna Boy

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, September 12, 2024

- Nigerian singer Burna Boy has revealed why he has not had a child yet

- The singer explained that he would not be having a child until he is confident that he can settle down

- Burna Boy noted that he wants to be a good parent like his parents are to him and his siblings

Popular Nigerian singer Damini Ebunoluwa Ogulu, professionally known as Burna Boy, has revealed why he has not had any child with any woman like his colleagues in the music industry. The singer shared his reason on the popular micro-blogging platform, Twitter.

The singer explained his reason for not having a child after a Twitter user identified as @Khalipha_Don asked him.

@Khalipa_Don asked the singer why he is not motivated to have a child. The Twitter user also asked the singer if he has a child already that people do not know about.
