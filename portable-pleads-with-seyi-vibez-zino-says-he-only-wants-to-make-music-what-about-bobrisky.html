Portable Pleads With Seyi Vibez, Zino, Says He Only Wants to Make Music: &quot;What About Bobrisky?&quot;

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, September 14, 2024
  • Controversial Nigerian singer Habeeb Okikiola, aka Portable, is trending again following a video he made
  • In the clip, the singer appeared to be begging for the forgiveness of his colleagues Zinolessky and Seyi Vibez
  • He noted that he was only interested in making music and money and that he had nothing but love for both of them

Street singer Portable, also known as Zazu, has extended an olive branch to his entertainment industry colleagues and Sam Larry.

This came days after the hitmaker was arrested over a debt of N14 million for one of his luxury rides.

Portable's message to Zinoleesky and Seyi Vibez

Usually, Portable goes on Instagram Live with his crew to rant and call out others, but this time was different. The street singer extended an olive branch to Nigerian Afro-fusion crooner Seyi Vibez and Marlain record signee Zinoleesky.
