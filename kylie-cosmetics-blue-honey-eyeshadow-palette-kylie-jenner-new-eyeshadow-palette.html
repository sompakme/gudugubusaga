Kylie Cosmetics Blue Honey Eyeshadow Palette- Kylie Jenner New Eyeshadow Palette

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, August 13, 2024

Kylie Jenner has built a near-billion dollar makeup empire in just a few short years. In that time, we've come to expect a familiar formula from the makeup mogul: every new product drop gets teased for a few days, revealed on Snapchat, and then swatched by Jenner herself. But the new Kylie Cosmetics Eyeshadow Palette called Blue Honey marks the first time Jenner, who is reportedly pregnant with her first child, hasn't teased or promoted the product on her personal Instagram or Snapchat account.

With over 15 million followers on the Kylie Cosmetics Instagram page, it's not exactly like Jenner needs to do promotion for every product on her personal account. Plus, with the birth of her child rumored to be happening very soon, she's got bigger things to worry about at the moment. But back to the palette.

Related Story

The new Kylie Cosmetics Blue Honey shadow palette has nine shades in exactly the color scheme you'd expect from the name: warm orange and pink tones and cool blues and teals. It's a surprising color combination that will satisfy both color-shy and bold makeup lovers.

Along with the palette come four new Velvet lipstick formulas in warm shades called Shook, Vibe, Heat, and Mood. All thew new products will be dropping on KylieCosmetics.com on January 23rd.

There is no word yet on whether Jenner is also planning on releasing a Valentine's Day collection like the fan-favorite collection from last year.
