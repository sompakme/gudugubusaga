Is Saint-14 dead or alive?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, August 5, 2024

Saint-14 is alive.

How did Saint-14 live?

Saint-14 was saved by a Guardian who repelled the Fallen forces and rescued him.

Does Saint-14 have his ghost?

Yes, Saint-14’s ghost is located in the Pools of Luminance in the Cistern.

How many vex did Saint-14 kill?

Saint-14 killed 1,304,512 vex and 101,511 fallen.

How long was Saint-14 stuck in the Infinite Forest?

Saint-14 was trapped in the Infinite Forest for centuries until the Vex created a machine to drain his Light.

Who is the strongest Hunter in destiny lore?

Lady Efrideet is considered the oldest and most powerful Hunter in Destiny lore.

Is Saint-14 a human or exo?

Saint-14 is an Exo Titan known for his crusade against the Fallen.

Who killed Vex?

Vex’ahlia was killed by a trap triggered by Percy, a member of Vox Machina.

Does Vex stay dead?

Vex was revived after a series of ability checks and offerings.

Who saved Saint-14?

A Guardian came to Saint-14’s rescue and repelled the Fallen forces.

Who did Saint-14 kiss?

Saint-14 kissed Osiris.

Who does Saint-14 love?

Saint-14 loved Osiris, not knowing that it was actually the Witch Queen pulling the strings.

How strong is Saint-14 in lore?

Saint-14 is considered one of the most powerful Lightbearers to ever live, having fought the Vex for countless years.

How old is Saint-14?

Saint-14 is at least 200 years old, but his time in the Infinite Forest may have added more years to his life.

What is Saint 14’s accent?

Saint-14 has a Russian accent, according to Bungie.

Was Saint-14 in the Battle of Twilight Gap?

Yes, Saint-14 fought in the Battle of Twilight Gap and other legendary battles of the Last City.

Does Percy marry Vex?

There is no information available about a marriage between Percy and Vex.

Is Vax dead?

Vax’ildan offered his life in service to The Raven Queen to save his sister, and after his death, he was brought back to life by her.

Is Vex a girl?

Vex is a female character portrayed as having a malevolent personality.

Is Vex half human?

There is no information available to suggest that Vex is half human.

What animal is Vex?

Vex is a Yordle, a being from the Spirit Realm.

What is Saint-14’s ghost name?

Saint-14’s ghost is named Geppetto.

Can exos kiss?

Exos have the ability to feel and produce warmth, allowing them to engage in activities like kissing.

Can you get Saint-14 armor?

Yes, the Saint-14 Armor is obtainable through the Guardian Cosmicube in Among Us.
