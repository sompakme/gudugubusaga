Early Life and Career Beginnings

Jhené Aiko was born and raised in Los Angeles, California. She began her music career contributing vocals to several B2K releases in the early 2000s. Aiko’s debut mixtape, “Sailing Soul(s)”, was released in 2011, which led to her signing with Artium and Def Jam Recordings.

Breakthrough and Success

Aiko’s breakthrough came with the release of her debut EP, “Sail Out”, in 2013. The EP spawned several hit singles, including “The Worst”, which peaked at number nine on the Billboard Hot 100. Her debut album, “Souled Out”, was released in 2014 and debuted at number three on the Billboard 200.

Continued Success and Recognition

Aiko’s success continued with the release of her second album, “Trip”, in 2017. The album debuted at number five on the Billboard 200 and was certified gold by the RIAA. Aiko has also received several award nominations, including three Grammy nominations.
