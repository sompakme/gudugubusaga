Who is Yolanda Andrade?

Yolanda Josefina Andrade Gomez professionally known as Yolanda Andrade is a Mexican actor and television presenter.

She began her career with the telenovela Yo no creo en los hombres in 1991 acting along with Alfredo Adame and Gabriela Roel.

Working on this project with such two great actors opened the doors to prove acting in different bigger productions. She got to work in bigger productions including Las Secrets Intenciones with Cristiano Castro in 1992.

Yolanda Andrade Career

The first opera in which Andrade appeared after completing her studies was the soap opera Yo no creo en los hombres. Her next role name was Larissa. She acted as Larissa in Las Secretas Castro.

She rose to unexpected fame after doing this project Andrade obtained the TvyNovelas award as a best young actress.

She continued working in soap operas until she received her first leading role. Her first leading role was in Buscando el Paraiso in 1993 working with Alex Ibarra and Karla Alvares.

Her second project was in the year 1995 which was Sentimientos. In this project, she got to work with Carlos Ponce and Chantal Andere.

Then Andrade was seen acting in Los Hijos de Nadie alongside Ramon Abascal and Silvia Derbez in 1997. In the same year, she was observed participating in a Mexican movie.

On March 1, 2000, Andrade stepped into hosting and she was seen hosting the show Hijas de la Madre Tierra with her friend Montserrat Oliver.

The show went very successful and owing to being a specialization of the show, they did a special transmission in Sydney, Australia from the 2000 Olympics.

Andrade like many other celebrities stepped into Big Brother VIP in the year 2003 where she was ranked on second place after the winner of the season Omar Chaparro.
