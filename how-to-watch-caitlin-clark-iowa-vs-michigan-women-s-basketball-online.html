How to Watch Caitlin Clark Iowa vs. Michigan Women's Basketball Online

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, August 11, 2024

Caitlin Clark seems poised to make history tonight, as the Iowa Hawkeyes host the Michigan Wolverines in an NCAA women’s basketball matchup.

Clark is just eight points shy of breaking the all-time women’s NCAA scoring record of 3,527 points, held by Washington’s Kelsey Plum — and tonight could be her night. Here’s what to know about Clark’s run for the record books.

Iowa vs. Michigan Women’s Basketball: Date, Time

The Iowa vs. Michigan women’s basketball game takes place tonight, Thursday, Feb. 15, at 5 p.m. PT/8 p.m. ET from Carver-Hawkeye Arena in Iowa City.
