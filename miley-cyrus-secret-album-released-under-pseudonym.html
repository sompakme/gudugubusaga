Miley Cyrus' secret album released under pseudonym

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, August 9, 2024

It is not the first time, nor will it be the last time, that a singer, actor or writer hides under a false name when publishing or performing a song. In the world of literature, it is very common for writers to submit their publications for prizes under a pseudonym to hide their identity so that the judges cannot judge the work by the name of the writer but rather by the content of the work.

The same is true in the world of film. There are many occasions when a well-known actor, recognized and even awarded with an award, has been characterized in such a way that it was impossible to know their true identity. In fact, in the credits of the film they do not even appear with their real name and it is only when they themselves confess it that their participation and playing a certain character comes to light and the details are known.

Hannah Montana, Miley Cyrus and now Clara Pierce

The controversial Miley Cyrus began her musical career with Disney. It was in the entertainment and teen series venture that she shot to stardom as an innocent Hanna Montana. Her songs and choreographies were seen by millions of people all over the world, but when she grew tired of that character (or rather, grew in mentality and physique) what seems to be the real character came to light, Miley took off her mask and became a woman on the cover of many magazines, opened news broadcasts and her music won over many fans who saw how the little girl became a woman in a matter of months.

This year the singer released her ninth studio album 'Endless Summer Vacation', where the single 'Flowers' quickly reached the top of the charts. However, what happened with that release left no one unmoved, as many thought that on the same day in March the artist had released another album, but under an unknown identity.

But it didn't go unnoticed by fans as, with a verified Spotify account and 244 monthly listeners so far, the artist named Clara Pierce only had one studio album of 12 tracks, but the album cover and the content of the songs with tracks like 'Hands of Time' and 'I Don't Want To Let It Go', are too similar to the Tennessee singer's style.

This discovery has generated so much noise on social media as many of them suggest that Clara Pierce, is a secret of Miley, that is to say, herself singing similar songs, but without revealing her true identity.
