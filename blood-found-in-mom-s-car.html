Blood Found in Moms Car

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, July 28, 2024

The case of missing mother Krista Dittmeyer took a grim turn Tuesday when police sources reported that they had found blood in the car she abandoned with her 14-month-old daughter inside. Authorities now fear that Dittmeyer, 20, was abducted. Her car was found four days ago in Conway, New Hampshire, with its engine and lights on and her daughter Aaliyah strapped into her car seat. Dittmeyer’s family says she doesn’t have a history of mental illness or depression, and police are investigating a second abandoned car that may be related to the case.
