What is Captain America's shield made of?

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, January 1, 0001
It is made of a unique Vibranium-metal alloy that has never been duplicated. The shield was cast by American metallurgist Dr. Myron MacLain, who was contracted by the U.S. government to create an impenetrable substance to use for tanks during World War II.

Is Captain America's shield Vibranium or proto adamantium?

Which is stronger Vibranium or adamantium?

While Adamantium is denser, it can still succumb to extreme heat, while Vibranium's only true weakness is sonic waves and itself. By lacking easily accessible counters, Vibranium easily takes the crown as the stronger metal.
