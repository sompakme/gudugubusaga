Nickelodeon Kids' Choice Sports Awards 2018: Complete List of Winners

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, October 2, 2024

The awards were handed out Thursday night in Santa Monica.

July 19, 2018 10:18pm

Modified on July 23, 2018, Published on July 19, 2018

The 2018 Kids' Choice Sports Awards were handed out Thursday night at Barker Hangar in Santa Monica.

A full list of winners follows.
