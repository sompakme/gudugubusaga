Angry WWE Fans Raid The Bumps Live Chat With Fire Vince McMahon

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, August 3, 2024

Vince McMahon is back in WWE and there’s nothing we can do about that, but some fans are trying. #FireVince is trending on Twitter for a reason, and those angry fans took it to The Bump’s chatroom in order to really let their voices be heard.

The Bump is still a destination for exclusive WWE Superstar interviews, and other live segments. They also have a live chat, and today, fans decided to give them something that they really can’t talk about on the show.

Tons of fans started spamming the chat with “Fire Vince,” which is also becoming a popular chant at events. WWE fans at NXT last night were event chanting it, which is not a good look.

As Ringside News exclusively reported, Endeavor is well aware that fans are upset that Vince McMahon is coming back. The fact is that they can’t back out of this billion-dollar deal now.

Vince McMahon is calling the shots, and he made many changes to WWE RAW during the show this week. People aren’t happy about this all over, and a few top Superstars have said that they plan to request their release from the company.
