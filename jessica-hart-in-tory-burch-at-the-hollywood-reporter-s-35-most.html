Jessica Hart in Tory Burch at The Hollywood Reporter's 35 Most

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, September 29, 2024

The Dress: A floral mesh dress and tassel sandals, both designed by Tory Burch.

The Wearer: Australian model Jessica Hart.

The Event: At The Hollywood Reporter‘s star-studded party, honoring the 35 Most Powerful People in New York Media on Wednesday evening. The 30-year-old beauty joined honorary guests Lena Dunham, Jenni Konner, Anna Wintour and Megyn Kelly, to name a few, at the Four Seasons restaurant’s Pool Room.

Why We Dig: Despite the chilly forecast in New York last night, Hart still managed to usher in a warm vibe with her springtime-friendly frock (currently available at toryburch.com for $895) and white tasseled shoes. The model maintained a casual look with her center-parted beach wave curls and minimal makeup, letting her outfit be the real standout. Let’s just hope the weather warms up so she can take it out for another spin in the Big Apple.
