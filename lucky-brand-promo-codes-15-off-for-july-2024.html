FAQs

Does Lucky Brand have free shipping?

Lucky Brand has free standard shipping on orders of $85 or more. This is only for US contiguous states. Your order should arrive within 3-6 working days. Standard shipping rates will be calculated at the checkout if your order is less than $85. You can always check this page for a Lucky Brand free shipping code too.  

Does Lucky Brand have sales?

Yes, Lucky Brand has sales throughout the year in its dedicated sales section with discounts of up to 75% on Lucky Brand jeans, dresses, and more. You'll also find some of the best savings during their Black Friday event and their seasonal sales. With occasional deals such as $25 off $75, Lucky Brand offers saving opportunities outside of their sales too!

Is there a Lucky Brand newsletter discount?

Sign up for the Lucky Brand newsletter and you’ll get 15% off your first purchase. You’ll also be updated on the latest sales and clothing from Lucky Brand. To sign up, go to the Lucky Brand website and scroll until you see the sign-up box, enter your preferred email address, and wait for your Lucky Brand code to arrive.  

Where can I find a Lucky Brand student discount?

Lucky Brand has a student discount of 15% off at this time. You will need to verify your student status with your educational email address to get your discount. You can do this by signing into Student Beans.  

Can I stack Lucky Brand coupons?

No. Lucky Brand only allows one coupon per order.  

What is the return policy at Lucky Brand?

You may return your online purchase within 60 days of your order date. Your items should be unwashed and unworn, with any tags still attached. Final sale items cannot be returned. Return shipping costs of $8 will be deducted from your refund total. Check the list of return exclusions from the Lucky Brand website before making your return.
