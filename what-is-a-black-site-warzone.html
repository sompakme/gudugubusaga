What is a Black Site warzone?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, September 2, 2024

A Black Site in Warzone is a challenging version of a Stronghold that offers excellent rewards in exchange for a firefight against heavily armored foes. It is a secret location within the game where players can participate in secret military and defense operations.

What does Black Site do?

Black Sites are locations that are never publicly acknowledged as existing, where secret military and defense operations, including the detention and interrogation of suspected terrorists, are conducted out of reach of the law and normal systems of oversight.

How do you get the Black Site in warzone?

To get a Black Site in Warzone, you need to complete a Stronghold by finding a Warzone 2 Stronghold key. These keys can be obtained by completing certain objectives or looting containers in the game. Once you have the key, you can access the Black Site.
